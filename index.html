Food Recipes Along With The Necessary Ingredients

1. Sambar (Lentil and Vegetable Stew)

Ingredients: 1 cup toor dal (pigeon peas) 1 medium carrot, diced 1 small potato, diced 1/2 cup drumstick (moringa) pieces 1/2 cup pumpkin, diced 1 onion, chopped 2 tomatoes, chopped 2 green chilies, slit 1 tablespoon tamarind pulp 1 tablespoon sambar powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon mustard seeds 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 dried red chili 2 tablespoons oil Salt to taste Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

2. Avial (Mixed Vegetable Curry with Coconut)

Ingredients: 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato, raw banana, and drumstick), chopped 1/2 cup grated fresh coconut 2 green chilies 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 tablespoon yogurt (optional) 1 tablespoon coconut oil 1 sprig curry leaves Salt to taste Water as needed

3. Kootu (Vegetable and Lentil Curry)

Ingredients: 1/2 cup moong dal (yellow split lentils) 1 medium potato, diced 1/2 cup pumpkin, diced 1/2 cup carrots, diced 1/2 cup beans, chopped 1 onion, chopped 1/2 cup grated coconut 1 teaspoon black pepper 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds 1 sprig curry leaves 1 tablespoon ghee or oil Salt to taste

4. Cabbage Thoran (Stir-Fried Cabbage with Coconut)

Ingredients: 1 medium cabbage, finely shredded 1/2 cup grated coconut 1 onion, chopped 2 green chilies, chopped 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds 1 sprig curry leaves 1 tablespoon oil Salt to taste

5. Rasam (South Indian Spicy Soup)

Ingredients: 1 cup tamarind extract 1/2 cup tomatoes, chopped 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds 2 dried red chilies 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 tablespoon rasam powder 1 sprig curry leaves 1 teaspoon ghee or oil Salt to taste Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
